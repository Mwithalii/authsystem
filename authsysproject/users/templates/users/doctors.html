Obstetrician

A compassionate and experienced obstetrician who specializes in high-risk pregnancies. Dr. Michael Rodriguez

Gynaecologist

A skilled gynecologist and fertility specialist. Dr. Rodriguez is known for his empathetic approach and his commitment to guiding his patients through the emotional and physical challenges of fertility treatments.

Pediatric
