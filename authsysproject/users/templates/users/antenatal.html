** T.T Istructions/Notes

All the antenatal clients should be asked about the number of tetanus toxoid injections they have received in their life to date - including those given after injuries and throughout schools.

This forms part of the 5 TTs. If none given, start as follows:

1. TT1 - Give to primigravida or on first contact

2. TT2 - Give not less than 4 weeks after TT1

3. Give during the 2nd pregnancy, any time before 8 months of pregnancy
